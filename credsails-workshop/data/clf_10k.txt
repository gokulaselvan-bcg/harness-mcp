ITEM 1A. RISK FACTORS AND CREDIT RATINGS (excerpt)

The steel industry is highly cyclical and sensitive to automotive demand, raw-material
costs, and global overcapacity. A downturn could materially affect results.

Collateral: borrowings under the ABL facility are secured by a first-priority lien on
inventory and receivables; the senior guaranteed notes are secured on a junior basis.

Credit ratings: the Company's senior notes are rated by S&P Global Ratings and Moody's.
As of the filing date the corporate family / issuer rating was in the "BB" category
(non-investment grade). Ratings are opinions of third-party agencies and may change.
